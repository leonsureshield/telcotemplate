Email Security Posture

A critical and pervasive failure across the industry

80%
Exposed
DMARC Missing/p=none (80%)
DMARC Secured (20%)
Critical DMARC Deficiency Critical
80% of telcos either completely lack a DMARC record or have a non-enforcing p=none policy — meaning emails can be spoofed with zero enforcement. Attackers can impersonate any of these telcos' domains to target their customers.
Soft-Fail SPF Records High Risk
60% of telcos use a soft-fail SPF record (~all) which still allows unauthorized senders to pass email checks. Combined with the DMARC gap, this creates an open door for spear-phishing campaigns targeting downstream telco customers at scale.
Business Impact
With 80% of telcos exposed, attackers can create convincing fake billing portals, network alerts, and customer service emails using legitimate telco domains — with high deliverability rates because the domain is trusted by spam filters and email clients.
Risk to Telco Customers: Domain spoofing, identity theft, spear-phishing targeting consumer and business subscribers at scale.

Methodology

How this data was collected and validated

This report is built on a rigorous 4-step data collection framework — from FCC source extraction through domain-level attack surface scanning, cross-referenced with CVE and KEV databases and enriched with business intelligence for segmented analysis.

Download Full Methodology →
01

FCC Registry Extraction

Downloaded and compiled all 7,321 FCC-registered carriers across 18 principal communication types. Removed closed operators and deduplicated FRNs — yielding 5,048 active telcos.

02

Attack Surface Scanning

Domain-level scans performed across all 5,048 telcos — assessing open ports, CVEs, SSL/TLS configuration, DNS records, subdomain mapping, DMARC/SPF posture, and dangling CNAMEs.

03

Vulnerability Intelligence Enrichment

All discovered CVEs cross-referenced against NIST NVD for CVSS scoring and CISA KEV for active exploitation status. EPSS scores used to assess weaponization likelihood.

04

Business Profile Construction

15+ business profile fields collected per telco using publicly available data and jeeva.ai — enabling revenue, size, geography, and sector-based segmentation for targeted analysis.

05

Statistical Analysis & Inference

Findings aggregated across segments to produce industry-level risk rates, exposure percentages, and threat category distributions. All figures based on the 3,204 successfully scanned domains.
